Devil's Monument — Bruce Peninsula
1.5 hrs Flowerpot formation Moderate trail Steep + rocky · grip shoes
  • Park at the end of Borchardt Rd, Northern Bruce Peninsula — free parking
  • Largest flowerpot rock formation on the Bruce Peninsula — formed 5,500 years ago
  • Short but steep rocky trail (~680m to the monument) — grip shoes essential
  • Stairs down to a gorgeous Georgian Bay cobble beach — swim if weather allows
  • Minhinnick Side Trail loop (~1 hr) to return — watch for poison ivy
  • Much quieter than the Grotto · stunning turquoise bay view from the top
